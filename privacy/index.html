We don't sell your data. We don't require an account. We use standard analytics to understand traffic and display ads to keep the site free. That's it. 1. Information we collect Information you provide directly This website does not require you to create an account or submit any personal information to use the tipping calculator or read the guides. If you contact us via email, we collect only the email address and message content you provide. Information collected automatically When you visit this site, certain information is collected automatically through standard web technologies: • Log data: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and date/time of your visit. • Usage data: How you interact with the calculator and which sections of the site you visit, collected through Google Analytics. • Device information: Screen size, device type, and browser version — used to ensure the site displays correctly on your device. • Location data: General geographic location (country, state, or city level) inferred from your IP address. We do not collect precise GPS location. 2. How we use your information We use the automatically collected information for the following purposes: • To understand how visitors use the site so we can improve content and functionality • To monitor site performance and fix technical issues • To serve relevant advertisements through our advertising partners • To comply with legal obligations • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive use of the site We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for email marketing. We do not build individual user profiles for any purpose other than the aggregate analytics described above. 3. Cookies and tracking This site uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. Essential cookies Required for the site to function. They store your preferences (such as calculator settings) temporarily in your browser session. No personal information is stored. Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. The data is anonymous and aggregated; we cannot identify individual users. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Advertising cookies Our advertising partners use cookies to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. These cookies may track your browsing activity across websites. See Section 4 for opt-out options. Managing cookies You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this site and others you visit. 4. Advertising This website displays advertisements to support free access to the content. Google AdSense / Google Ad Manager Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info. Ads served on this site are clearly labeled as advertisements. We do not accept payment to include products or services in our editorial content. The tipping advice on this site is editorially independent. 5.
